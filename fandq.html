How do I check out DVDs from the Jones Media Center?

Simply, write down the DVD's call number and take it to the Jones Media circulation desk. One of our staff members will retrieve the DVD(s) from our collection.

How long is the loan period?

For students and staff, the loan period is 3 days. For faculty, the loan period is 7 days.

What is the late fee?

The late fee is $1 per day per item.

What does it mean when an item is "on reserve"?

The item is on reserve for a class and it can only be watched in the media center.

How many DVDs can I check out at once?

Students and staff can checkout a maximum of 3 items. Faculty members can check out an unlimited number of items.

Can I place an item on hold?

Items can only be placed on hold for faculty members.
