How do I check out DVDs from the Jones Media Center?

Simply, write down the DVDs call number to the Jones Media circulation desk and one of our staff members will retrieve the DVDs from our collection.

How long is the loan period?

For students and staff, the loan period is 3 days. For faculty, the loan period is 7 days.

What is the late fee?

The late fee is $1 per day per item.
